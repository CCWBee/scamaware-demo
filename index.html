Know the threats.

Scammers in Jersey use these common tactics. Learn to recognise them.

🏢

Bank Impersonation

Texts or calls claiming your account is compromised. They'll ask you to move money to a "safe" account — which belongs to them.

📦

Delivery Fees

A text says you need to pay a small fee to receive a parcel. The link steals your card details.

📈

Investment Fraud

Promises of guaranteed returns on crypto or overseas investments. If it sounds too good to be true, it is.

🏛

Tax Scams

Calls claiming to be from Treasury & Exchequer about unpaid taxes, threatening arrest unless you pay immediately.

💕

Romance Scams

Fake relationships built over weeks or months, ending with urgent requests for money. Jersey loses over £400k annually.

💻

Tech Support

Calls claiming your computer has a virus. They'll ask for remote access, then steal your data or install malware.

Red flags to watch for.

Scammers rely on urgency and emotion. Pause and look for these signs.

⚠

Urgency & Pressure

"Act now or your account will be closed" — real organisations don't do this.

💳

Unusual Payments

Gift cards, crypto, or wire transfers. Legitimate companies don't ask for these.

🔒

Password Requests

Your bank will never ask for your PIN or full password. Ever.

🤫

"Keep This Secret"

Scammers isolate victims. If they say don't tell anyone, tell someone.

🎁

Too Good to Be True

Won a lottery you didn't enter? Unexpected inheritance? It's a scam.

📱

Remote Access

Never let anyone you don't know take control of your computer.
