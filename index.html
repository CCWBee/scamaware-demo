Common Scam Types in Jersey

🏢

Bank Impersonation

Fraudsters pretending to be from your bank, asking you to "verify" your account or move money to a "safe" account.

💼

JFSC Impersonation

Scammers claiming to be from the Jersey Financial Services Commission demanding immediate action.

📦

Delivery Fee Scams

Fake texts claiming you need to pay a fee to receive a parcel, often impersonating Jersey Post.

📈

Investment Fraud

Too-good-to-be-true investment opportunities, often involving cryptocurrency or overseas schemes.

💕

Romance Scams

Fake online relationships that eventually lead to requests for money. Jersey loses over 400k annually.

💻

Tech Support Scams

Calls claiming to be from Microsoft or Apple, saying your computer has a virus and needs fixing.

Red Flags to Watch For

⚠ Urgency or pressure to act NOW
💳 Gift cards or crypto payments
🔒 Requests for passwords or PINs
🤫 "Keep this secret"
🎁 Too good to be true offers
📱 Requests for remote access
